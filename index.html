Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Revolution NFT?

+

Digital Revolution NFT is a unique collection of 608 digital artworks that celebrate the milestones of technological advancement. Each piece is carefully crafted and verified on the blockchain.

How do I purchase an NFT?

+

To purchase an NFT, you'll need a MetaMask wallet and some BNB. Connect your wallet, select your desired NFT, and click the "Buy NFT" button to start the minting process.

What blockchain is used?

+

We use the OpenBNB blockchain for its efficiency, low gas fees, and environmental sustainability.

What are the benefits of holding?

+

Holders get exclusive access to future drops, private Discord community, staking rewards, and governance rights in our DAO.

Is there a rarity system?

+

Yes, our NFTs have four rarity levels: Common (70%), Rare (20%), Epic (7%), and Legendary (3%). Each level has unique traits and benefits.

Can I sell my NFT?

+

Yes, you can sell your NFT on any OpenBNB-compatible marketplace. We recommend using our partner marketplace for the best experience.
